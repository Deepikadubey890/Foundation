We request you to be a part of this campaign and help us to prevent the plastic from going to the garbage. Other aim is to make people aware regarding the environmental protection. It has also involve short-term and long-term measures. Awareness of environmental protection can be increased by many ways like:- environmental campaigns, by the help of media and social media and through classroom education, which could shows our responsibility towards environment protection. Finding solutions for the save biodiversity is our another aim.
